Places to Visit in Haryana

Kurukshetra

Kurukshetra, the land of the Mahabharata, is a significant pilgrimage site with numerous temples and historical landmarks.

Morni Hills

Morni Hills is a picturesque hill station known for its lush greenery, serene lakes, and adventurous trekking trails.

Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary

Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary is a haven for bird watchers, home to numerous migratory and resident bird species.

Kingdom of Dreams

Kingdom of Dreams in Gurugram offers a unique entertainment experience with live performances, cultural shows, and delicious cuisine.
