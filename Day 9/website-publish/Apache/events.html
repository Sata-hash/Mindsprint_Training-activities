Events in Haryana

Mango Mela

The annual Mango Mela in Pinjore is a delightful celebration of mangoes with cultural performances, food stalls, and fun activities.

Surajkund Mela

The Surajkund Mela is an internationally renowned crafts fair held in February, showcasing traditional arts, crafts, and cuisine from all over India.
